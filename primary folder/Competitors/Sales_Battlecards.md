# BATTLECARD: TEMENOS

## Quick Profile
- **Strength:** Global leader, 3000+ clients, comprehensive functionality
- **Weakness:** Expensive, slow implementation, poor local support
- **Threat Level:** HIGH for Tier 1 banks, LOW for others

## When You'll Compete
- Tier 1 bank RFPs
- Multi-country deployments  
- Banks wanting "safe" choice

## How to Win

### Discovery Questions:
1. "What's your budget for implementation AND ongoing support?"
2. "How important is local, on-site support?"
3. "What's your timeline to go live?"
4. "How much customization do you need for local requirements?"

### Key Differentiators:

| They Say | You Say |
|----------|---------|
| "Global leader with 3000+ clients" | "How many in Africa? We have 100+ here" |
| "Comprehensive functionality" | "Configured for Africa or Amsterdam?" |
| "Industry standard" | "Standard where? Mobile money? Agent banking?" |
| "Best technology" | "Best for whom? Ask their African clients about support" |

### Winning Messages:
✅ **"60% lower TCO over 5 years"** - Include all costs
✅ **"6 months vs 18 months to go-live"** - Faster value
✅ **"Local support included, not $1000/day"** - Real partnership
✅ **"Pre-configured for African regulations"** - No surprises
✅ **"Ask Bank X about their Temenos experience"** - Reference their struggles

### Proof Points:
- Operating national clearing houses (they don't)
- 30 years in Africa (they visit)
- Local offices in 8 countries (they have 0)
- Fixed price implementations (they don't offer)

### Trap Questions for Customer:
- "Can you afford EUR 5-10M for implementation?"
- "Do you have 18-24 months for the project?"
- "Will your team fly to Europe for training?"
- "How will you handle support at 2 AM local time?"

# UNIVERSAL OBJECTION HANDLERS

## "Competitor X is cheaper"
**Response:** "Cheaper initially or over 5 years? Let's calculate:"
- Implementation: They're cheaper? Include travel, customization
- Support: Include annual fees, change requests, upgrades
- Hidden costs: Training, integration, regulatory updates
- Risk cost: What if they fail? Switching costs?
- **Bottom line:** "We're 40% less expensive over 5 years"

## "Competitor X has better technology"
**Response:** "Better for Silicon Valley or Siaya County?"
- Our tech handles 70% of East African clearing
- Built for African reality: USSD, offline, mobile money
- Ask about uptime in low-bandwidth environments
- Technology that works here, proven here
- **Bottom line:** "Best technology for African reality"

## "Competitor X is the safe choice"
**Response:** "Safe for whom?"
- Safe for consultants billing daily rates?
- Safe for vendors with no local presence?
- We've operated here for 30 years
- We run national infrastructure
- **Bottom line:** "We're the safe African choice"

## "Competitor X implements faster"
**Response:** "Faster to what?"
- Pilot or production?
- Including migration?
- Including training?
- Including regulatory approval?
- **Bottom line:** "We guarantee production in 6 months"

# COMPETITIVE INTELLIGENCE CHECKLIST

## In Every Competitive Situation, Find Out:

### About the Competitor:
- [ ] Who's their local reference?
- [ ] What's their actual price (all-in)?
- [ ] Who's implementing (local or foreign)?
- [ ] What's their committed timeline?
- [ ] What are they NOT including?

### About the Customer:
- [ ] Previous vendor experiences?
- [ ] Budget reality vs. aspirations?
- [ ] Timeline pressure points?
- [ ] Who's the real decision maker?
- [ ] What's the #1 fear?

### About the Deal:
- [ ] Why now?
- [ ] What happens if they don't decide?
- [ ] Who else are they talking to?
- [ ] What's the selection criteria ranking?
- [ ] When do they decide?
