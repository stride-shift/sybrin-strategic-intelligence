## How to Verify the Data

1. **For current bank lists:** Visit each central bank's website and look for their "Supervised Institutions" page
2. **For market statistics:** Check the central bank's latest Financial Stability Report or Banking Sector Report
3. **For individual banks:** Visit their websites or download their annual reports
4. **For regulatory updates:** Check central bank circulars and press releases
