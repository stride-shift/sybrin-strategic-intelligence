# Jobs-to-be-Done Executive Summary & Sales Application Guide

## Executive Summary: Critical Jobs Insights

### The Big Picture
Sybrin's products don't just solve technical problems - they address fundamental human and organizational needs across three dimensions:

1. **Functional** - What customers need to GET DONE (efficiency, compliance, growth)
2. **Emotional** - How customers want to FEEL (confident, secure, in control)
3. **Social** - How customers want to BE PERCEIVED (innovative, compliant, trustworthy)

### Key Discovery: The Job Architecture

#### Surface Level (What Customers Say They Want)
- Process payments faster
- Onboard customers digitally
- Detect fraud
- Manage documents

#### Deeper Level (What They Really Need)
- Reduce operational costs by 30-50%
- Achieve unquestionable regulatory compliance
- Build competitive differentiation
- Enable business model innovation

#### Hidden Level (The Non-Obvious Jobs)
- **Attract and retain top talent** through modern technology
- **Signal innovation** to investors and markets
- **Create optionality** for future pivots
- **Build ecosystem platforms** for new revenue
- **Preserve institutional knowledge** through automation
- **Enable ESG compliance** and social impact

## Sales Playbook: How to Use JTBD

### 1. Discovery Questions by Job Type

#### For Functional Jobs:
- "What processes take the most time today?"
- "Where are your biggest cost centers?"
- "What's preventing you from scaling?"
- "Which regulations keep you up at night?"

#### For Emotional Jobs:
- "What concerns you most about the current system?"
- "How confident are you in your compliance?"
- "What would give you more peace of mind?"
- "Where do you feel most vulnerable?"

#### For Social Jobs:
- "How do you want to be positioned in the market?"
- "What would make you proud of your operation?"
- "How do regulators view your institution?"
- "What story do you want to tell the board?"

---

### 2. Position by Customer Context

#### For Banks Under Regulatory Pressure:
**Lead with:** Compliance and risk jobs
**Key Message:** "Achieve bulletproof compliance in 90 days"
**Proof Points:** Regulatory approval stories
**Bundle:** Complete compliance suite

#### For Cost-Pressured Institutions:
**Lead with:** Operational excellence jobs
**Key Message:** "Reduce operations cost by 30% in year one"
**Proof Points:** ROI calculations, efficiency metrics
**Bundle:** Automation and efficiency package

#### For Digital Transformation Initiatives:
**Lead with:** Transformation and experience jobs
**Key Message:** "Transform without disruption"
**Proof Points:** Transformation success stories
**Bundle:** Digital excellence platform

#### For Innovation-Focused Banks:
**Lead with:** Growth and ecosystem jobs
**Key Message:** "Build tomorrow's bank today"
**Proof Points:** Innovation examples, new revenue streams
**Bundle:** Innovation enablement suite

## Objection Handling Through JTBD

### "It's too expensive"
**Reframe to Job:** "What's the cost of NOT solving [regulatory compliance/operational efficiency/customer loss]?"

### "We have other priorities"
**Reframe to Job:** "Which is more urgent: [current priority] or [preventing regulatory fines/stopping customer churn/reducing costs by 30%]?"

### "Our current system works fine"
**Reframe to Job:** "It may work, but is it helping you [win new customers/reduce costs/stay compliant with new regulations]?"

### "We need to think about it"
**Reframe to Job:** "While you're thinking, you're still [losing $X daily/at risk of compliance issues/losing customers to competitors]"
