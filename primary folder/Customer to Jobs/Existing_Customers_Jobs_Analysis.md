# Sybrin's Existing Customers: Why They Hired Sybrin
## Jobs-to-be-Done Analysis of Current Customer Base

---

## Executive Summary

This analysis examines why Sybrin's existing customers chose their solutions, revealing the specific jobs these institutions needed to accomplish. With dominant market positions in Kenya (56%), Rwanda (64%), and significant presence in Tanzania (30%) and Zambia (35%), Sybrin's success stems from addressing critical infrastructure and transformation jobs that competitors couldn't fulfill.

### Key Insights:
- **Infrastructure operators** (clearing houses) hired Sybrin for national-scale reliability
- **Regional banks** hired Sybrin for multi-country standardization
- **Market leaders** hired Sybrin for ecosystem orchestration capabilities
- **Trust and local presence** trump features in African markets
